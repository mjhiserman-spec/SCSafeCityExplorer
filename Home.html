Flock Safety Spend Analysis

Annual Flock Safety Investment: $450,000/year
40 ALPR cameras, 15 Live Video cameras, 10 Raven sensors, FlockOS, DFR pilot, Flock911.
ROI: 30-40% case clearance increase, 50-70% investigation time reduction, multi-department value.
At only 5.3% of Police capital budget, delivers coverage and insight equal to multiple officer positions.

Safe City Analysis & Strategic Opportunities

Deployment Opportunities:
  • Law Enforcement – City-wide ALPR, RTCC integration
  • Fire – DFR drones, Live Video, Flock911 dispatch
  • Parks – Video for public spaces, Freeform for events
  • Traffic & City Planning – Data-driven intersection monitoring
