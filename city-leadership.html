Elected Officials
  • Mayor
    Lisa M. Gillmor
    Fourth-generation Santa Claran, real estate broker, former council member, and vice mayor. Champions infrastructure improvements, housing balance, neighborhood preservation, diversity, and economic development. Major force in committee leadership and political advocacy, aiming to protect Santa Clara’s identity while guiding big growth.
    Committee Affiliations: Chairs Task Force on Diversity, Equity & Inclusion. Serves ex officio on Sports & Open Space Authority, Stadium Authority, Industrial Development Authority, and others.
    Initiatives & Interests: Small town feel with big opportunities, ethics in government, public safety, downtown revitalization, responsible stadium operations, affordable housing.
    Current Value from Flock: Flock Safety supports safe growth downtown and near event sites (Levi's Stadium). Enables real-time evidence collection, deters vehicle theft, and supports business safety.
    Strategic Expansion Value: More Flock cameras and analytics in targeted growth districts will bolster safety during large events, reinforce traffic & pedestrian monitoring, and support neighborhood preservation—the mayor’s core goals.

    Key Initiatives: Real-Time Crime Center, Organized Retail Theft Task Force (*with DA*), Technology Innovation Team, Patrol and Investigations modernization.
